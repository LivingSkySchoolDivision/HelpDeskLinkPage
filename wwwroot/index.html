New (Aug 2021): The help desk portal does not use your standard Living Sky account anymore.

  • On your first visit, you will need to Sign up.
  • Use your Living Sky email account to sign up.
  • An account is not required to send in tickets via email.
    • You can use email to create and reply to tickets without making an account.
  • An account allows you to see and track all of your tickets in the same place.

Forgotten Help Desk passwords

  • Go to the login page.
  • Enter your email address.
  • Click Next.
  • Click Forgot your password?.
  • Check your email for password reset instructions.
